What to target

Garfish (hornfisk)

Garfish (hornfisk) are at their seasonal peak this month in the Øresund, and the 10 °C water sits right in their range. Sea trout (havørred) are the backup option if the garfish are quiet.

Full garfish guide on fishingindenmark.info →

Setup

Light spinning rod with 8–18 g slim spoons fished fast and high in the water — or a strip of mackerel under a casting float. The classic trick: a tuft of red wool above the hook, so their bony beaks tangle in it and you barely need the hook to hold them.

They cruise near the surface, often within a rod-length or two of the edge. Keep the lure shallow and the retrieve quick.

Backup: Sea trout (havørred). Work points, drop-offs and current edges. The dawn window is the one to fish hardest — first light is prime.
